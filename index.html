Short Bio

I am now a professor and doctoral supervisor at the School of Computer Science and Technology, Harbin Institute of Technology, and leader of the smart healthcare research team at Harbin Institute of Technology. I am selected for the HIT Young Top Talents, the Longjiang Science and Technology Talent Support Program (Heilongjiang Province High level Talent Program), and the first reserve leader of the Heilongjiang Province Professional and Technical Talent Team. I have been in the field of cardiac imaging analysis for over 10 years. I completed my Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering from East China Jiaotong University in 2012. I then received my Master's and Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Technology at Harbin Institute of Technology in 2014 and 2019, respectively, with Prof. Kuanquan Wang. During my doctoral studies, I was a joint Ph.D. student at Western University (2017-2018), mentored by Prof. Shuo Li. I am also a visiting scientist at King Abdullah University of Science and Technology from 2023 to 2024, working closely with Prof. Xin Gao. My research interests include Multimodal Medical Image Analysis and Artificial Intelligence Technology.
