Short Bio

I am now an Associate Professor and Ph.D./Master Supervisor at the School of Computer Science and Technology, at Harbin Institute of Technology. I completed my Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering from East China Jiaotong University in 2012. I then received my Master's and Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Technology at Harbin Institute of Technology in 2014 and 2019, respectively, with Prof. Kuanquan Wang. During my doctoral studies, I was a joint Ph.D. student at Western University (2017-2018), mentored by Prof. Shuo Li. My research interests include Multimodal Medical Image Analysis and Artificial Intelligence Technology.
